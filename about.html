About Tejas Agri Seeds

We are dedicated to empowering farmers with reliable, high-yielding vegetable seeds. Our portfolio is curated to deliver performance in Indian conditions—uniform growth, strong disease tolerance, and excellent shelf life—so you can harvest more with confidence.

Our Goals

  • Deliver certified, high-germination seeds across India.
  • Introduce improved hybrids and OPVs.
  • Promote sustainable, climate-smart farming practices.
  • Ensure farmer prosperity with quality and support.

Strategies

  • Field Demonstrations: On-ground trials to build trust.
  • Dealer Network: Strong distribution & timely supply.
  • Quality Assurance: Batch testing, moisture-proof packaging.
  • Farmer Education: Guides on sowing, irrigation & pest control.
  • Pricing & Offers: Combos, discounts, loyalty programs.
  • After-Sales Support: Helpline & WhatsApp assistance.
